FILMS — movie posters
=====================
Drop poster images here (portrait .jpg/.png work best).

To show a film on the Films tab, open revamp.html, find the
Films page (<div id="films">), copy one <section> block, then set:
  - the poster:  src="media/films/<yourfile>.jpg"
  - the title, the Letterboxd link, and the note.
No date/month dividers on this page.
