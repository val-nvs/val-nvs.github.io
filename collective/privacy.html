Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

Colectivo ("we", "us", "our") is a creative collective based in Lisbon, Portugal. We are committed to protecting your personal data in accordance with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR — Regulation (EU) 2016/679) and Portuguese data protection law (Lei n.º 58/2019).

Data Controller

Colectivo, Lisbon, Portugal. For any data protection enquiries, please contact us at: privacy@colectivo.pt

What Data We Collect

When you use our website, we may collect the following personal data: your name and email address (when you submit a booking form), your shipping address and payment information (when you make a purchase — processed securely by Stripe), and basic analytics data (page views, device type — anonymized, no cookies required).

How We Use Your Data

We use your data to process and fulfill your orders, respond to booking enquiries, send order confirmations and shipping updates, and improve our website and services. We do not use your data for marketing purposes unless you explicitly opt in.

Legal Basis

We process your data under the following legal bases as defined by GDPR Article 6: contractual necessity (to fulfill orders and bookings), legitimate interest (to improve our services), and consent (for any marketing communications, which you can withdraw at any time).

Payment Processing

All payments are processed by Stripe Payments Europe, Ltd. We do not store your credit card details, MB WAY information, or Multibanco references on our servers. Stripe's privacy policy applies to all payment data: stripe.com/privacy

Form Submissions

Booking forms are processed through Formspree or Web3Forms. These services receive your form data (name, email, message) and forward it to us via email. Both services are GDPR-compliant. Your form data is not stored permanently on third-party servers.

Cookies

This website does not use tracking cookies. We do not use Google Analytics or any third-party tracking scripts. If we integrate any cookie-based services in the future, we will update this policy and implement a cookie consent mechanism.

Your Rights

Under GDPR, you have the right to access your personal data, rectify inaccurate data, request erasure of your data ("right to be forgotten"), restrict or object to processing, data portability, and withdraw consent at any time. To exercise any of these rights, contact us at privacy@colectivo.pt. We will respond within 30 days.

Data Retention

We retain order data for 5 years for tax and legal compliance (as required by Portuguese law). Booking form data is retained for 12 months. You may request deletion at any time, subject to legal retention requirements.

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services: Stripe (payments), Formspree/Web3Forms (form handling), GitHub Pages (hosting), and Google Fonts (typography). Each has their own privacy policies which we encourage you to review.

Complaints

If you believe your data protection rights have been violated, you have the right to lodge a complaint with the Portuguese data protection authority: Comissão Nacional de Proteção de Dados (CNPD) — www.cnpd.pt

Livro de Reclamações Online: www.livroreclamacoes.pt

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date. We encourage you to review this page periodically.
