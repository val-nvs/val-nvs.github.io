About us.

We met in a darkroom in Alfama in 2022. It was an accident — two strangers developing film at 2am, arguing about push-processing Tri-X. That conversation turned into a collaboration, and the collaboration turned into Colectivo.

We're a small group of photographers, filmmakers, and makers based in Lisbon and the surrounding coast. We shoot on film because we believe in the process — the patience, the imperfection, the magic of chemistry and light.

We sell our prints because we think analog photography belongs on walls, not just on screens. We build camera accessories because the tools should be as personal as the work. And we shoot for clients because we love making beautiful things with other people.

Everything we do is rooted in craft. We develop our own film, print in our own darkroom, and design our 3D-printed accessories from scratch. No shortcuts, no compromises. Just the work.
