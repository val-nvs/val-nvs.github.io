Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

Colectivo ("we", "us") is a creative collective based in Lisbon, Portugal. We protect your personal data in accordance with GDPR (Regulation (EU) 2016/679) and Portuguese data protection law (Lei n.º 58/2019).

Data Controller

Colectivo, Lisbon, Portugal. Contact: privacy@colectivo.pt

What We Collect

Name and email (booking forms), shipping address and payment info (purchases via Stripe), and anonymized analytics (page views, device type — no cookies).

How We Use It

To fulfill orders, respond to bookings, send confirmations and shipping updates, and improve our services. No marketing unless you opt in.

Legal Basis

Contractual necessity (orders/bookings), legitimate interest (service improvement), and consent (marketing, withdrawable at any time).

Payment Processing

Payments are handled by Stripe. We never store card details, MB WAY info, or Multibanco references. See Stripe's privacy policy.

Forms

Booking forms use Formspree or Web3Forms (GDPR-compliant). Data is forwarded to us via email and not stored permanently on third-party servers.

Cookies

This website does not use tracking cookies or third-party analytics scripts.

Your Rights

Under GDPR you can access, rectify, erase, restrict, port, or object to processing of your data. Contact privacy@colectivo.pt — we respond within 30 days.

Retention

Order data: 5 years (Portuguese tax law). Booking data: 12 months. Deletion available on request, subject to legal requirements.

Third Parties

Stripe (payments), Formspree/Web3Forms (forms), GitHub Pages (hosting), Google Fonts (typography).

Complaints

CNPD — www.cnpd.pt

Livro de Reclamações Online — www.livroreclamacoes.pt
